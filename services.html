Our Mission

Safety First

At Inkonjane the safety of our employees is non-negotiable. We believe that working safely must be a way of life. This is demonstrated by numerous milestones being achieved on various projects.

We are committed to ensuring that our operations comply with the Occupational Health and Safety Act, the Compensation for Occupational injuries and Diseases Act, Environmental legislation.
